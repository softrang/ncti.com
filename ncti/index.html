Welcome to Narsingdi Computer Training Institute.


At Narsingdi Computer Training Institute, we believe in empowering individuals through technology and education. Located in the heart of Narsingdi, our institute has been a beacon of learning and skill development for many years, providing top-notch computer training to students of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and equip our students with the necessary skills to thrive in today's technology-driven world. We are committed to delivering high-quality education that is accessible, affordable, and aligned with industry standards.

Courses Offered

We offer a wide range of courses designed to meet the diverse needs of our students. Whether you're a beginner looking to gain basic computer skills or an advanced learner aiming to master specialized software, we have something for everyone. Our courses include:

  • Basic Computer Literacy: Introduction to computers, operating systems, and essential software applications.
  • Office Automation: Comprehensive training in Microsoft Office Suite, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.
  • Graphic Design: Learn the fundamentals of graphic design using popular tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and CorelDRAW.
  • Web Development: Master the art of creating dynamic websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular content management systems like WordPress.
  • Programming Languages: In-depth courses on programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, and more.
  • Digital Marketing: Understand the strategies and tools needed for effective online marketing, including SEO, social media marketing, and email marketing.
  • Hardware and Networking: Gain practical knowledge in computer hardware troubleshooting and network configuration.

Experienced Instructors

Our instructors are industry professionals with years of experience in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing personalized attention and guidance to ensure each student achieves their learning goals. With their expertise, our students receive practical insights and hands-on experience that prepare them for real-world challenges.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Narsingdi Computer Training Institute is equipped with modern facilities to enhance the learning experience. Our classrooms are fitted with the latest computers, software, and high-speed internet to ensure uninterrupted learning. We also provide access to a rich library of resources, including books, tutorials, and online materials.

Student Success Stories

Our alumni have gone on to achieve remarkable success in various industries, both locally and internationally. From securing jobs in top IT companies to launching their own tech startups, our students' accomplishments are a testament to the quality of education we provide.

Join Us Today

Take the first step towards a brighter future with Narsingdi Computer Training Institute. Whether you're looking to start a new career, advance in your current job, or simply explore a new hobby, our courses are designed to help you succeed.
